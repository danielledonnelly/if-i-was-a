IF I WAS A...

This project was inspired by my least favourite job interview question: if you were a cereal, what would you be? I was asked that many years ago and still think about it to this day.

I don't anticipate I'll be having any job interviews in the near future, but still wanted to make a list of "If I Was A..." prompts, just for fun.

Cereal

I would be Surreal because I’m sweet, sarcastic, and eccentric, with a keen eye for out-of-the-box marketing ideas.

If you have no idea what I’m talking about, look up “Surreal cereal advertisements” to familarize yourself with the best cereal brand of all time.

Tool

I would be a Swiss Army knife because I can do a bit of everything.

I'm a stellar writer with a great eye for design. I have a lot of experience in data and development as well. Much like a Swiss Army Knife, I can be excel at a wide variety of tasks!

Flower

I would be a blue hydrangea, because blue hydrangeas symbolize apologies, gratitude, and understanding.

I think that sums me up pretty well! While I am pretty resilient, just as hydrangea flowers are, I do tend to apologize a bit too much. I'm also extremely appreciative and understanding with the people around me.

Fruit

I would be a starfruit (also known as carambola) because I’m efficient, loyal, and friendly!

That might not make much sense without some additional context... In Stardew Valley, star fruits are the most efficient fruit to farm. In Kingdom Hearts, start-shaped fruit represent loyalty and lifelong friendship.

Animal

I would be be a cat because I'm inquisitive, introverted, and I love napping.

If I were smaller, I imagine I’d also love sleeping in boxes, lying down on keyboards, and knocking things off of countertops.

Color

I would be this specific shade of blue because I’m bright and fun, but there’s more to me than meets the eye.

Bright blue is often associated with creativity, concentration, and clarity, which matches me well!

Song

I would be Danny Don’t You Know by NSP because I'm a bit of a nerd.

I’m not going to elaborate much further, but if you look up the lyrics, I think you’ll get the picture!

Superhero

I would be the Flash because I like to work in fast-paced environments.

I’m quick, effective, and innovative. I value my time, and love finding new efficient ways to do everyday tasks.

Drink

I would be a soda because sometimes I’m bubbly and energized, while other times I’m chill and a bit more flat.

If I had to pick a specific brand or flavour, it would probably be Mountain Dew.

Creature

I would be an alien because I'm intelligent and highly curious.

I'm pretty interested in science, data, and technology. I haven't been on earth for all that long yet and I'm always eager to learn as much as I possibly can.
